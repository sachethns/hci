Frequently Asked Questions
How do I report an obstruction?▾
From the home screen, tap the red "Report Obstruction" button at the bottom of the map. Your GPS location is detected automatically. Select the type of obstruction, optionally add a photo or adjust the pin, and hit Submit. The whole process takes under 10 seconds.
How does safe routing work?▾
BonVoyage uses crowdsourced obstruction reports and historical intersection safety data to calculate routes. When you search for a destination, we show multiple route options ranked by safety. Each route shows the number of active obstructions and dangerous intersections.
What do the safety ratings mean?▾
Intersections are rated 1–5 based on cyclist-reported incidents, infrastructure quality (protected signals, bike lanes), and historical accident data. 1-2 stars means high risk, 3 is moderate, and 4-5 means the intersection is well-designed for cyclists.
When do reports expire?▾
Delivery vehicle and parked car reports expire after 2 hours. Construction reports last 24 hours. All reports can be confirmed or dismissed by other cyclists, which affects their visibility and lifespan.
How do I reduce alert fatigue?▾
Go to Alert Preferences from the sidebar menu. You can set intersection warnings to "High-Risk Only" so you're only alerted for the most dangerous spots. You can also toggle voice warnings on or off and enable auto-dismiss for popup alerts.
